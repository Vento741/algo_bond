## Key Insights

### 1. Trailing ATR=8 is dominant
All TOP-10 configs use `trailing_atr_mult=8`. Shorter trailing (4, 6) gets stopped out too early on SOL's volatility.

### 2. ADX=30 > ADX=20
Stricter ADX filter dramatically improves results by filtering low-quality signals in choppy markets.

### 3. RSI=55 > RSI=40
Relaxed RSI threshold allows more entries during moderate pullbacks, improving trade count and overall PnL.

### 4. SL=3.0-4.0 optimal
Wide stops prevent premature exits. SL=2.0 consistently loses money. SL=4.0 gives slightly better WR (41% vs 35%) but similar PnL.

### 5. Order size is a risk/reward slider
- os=30: +7.7% PnL, 10.4% DD (conservative)
- os=50: +12.4% PnL, 16.8% DD (aggressive)
DD scales roughly linearly with order size.

### 6. TP=10 is the sweet spot
TP=5 (too tight) — exits too early, negative PnL. TP=15 — slightly worse, allows more giveback.
